COVID-19 at a glance

Just how deeply has COVID-19 impacted the populations of different countries, and what factors contribute to a high mortality?

Comparing HDI with population deaths

From this diagram, we can see that there is a positive correlation where countries that are more developed, or have a higher human development index, somehow tend to have more deaths per million population. On the other hand, countries in poorer continents such as Africa and Asia tend to have a lower number of deaths per million population.
